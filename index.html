About

I am passionate PhD student in machine learning and software engineering, my research focuses on federated learning and the application of software engineering techniques as strengthening mechanisms.

My research

Since October 2024, I have been a PhD student at the University of Rennes, working within the DiverSE team at the Inria Centre of Rennes. The core of my research is federated learning, a decentralized machine learning paradigm that allows multiple parties to collaboratively train models without sharing raw data. I investigate how principles and techniques from software engineering—such as variability management and software testing—can be applied to strengthen the robustness and security of federated systems in the face of various attacks and failures. This work lies at the crossroads of artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and software engineering.
